A letter just for you

Hey Bro,

I don't say this nearly enough, but today gives me the excuse — and I'm taking it. Growing up alongside you was the best kind of accident the universe ever made. You have been my rival, my teammate, my confidant, and my constant, often all four in the same afternoon.

There's something nobody tells you about having a brother: it's not always the big dramatic moments that matter. It's the late-night conversations that went nowhere and everywhere. It's the look across the room that means everything without a single word. It's knowing that no matter how far the road takes either of us, you are always home to me.

So today, I'm not just wishing you a happy Brother's Day. I'm thanking you. For every moment — even the ones we argued through. Especially those.

You are, without question, one of the best things in my life. Don't forget that.

Krishna Your brother, always ✦

Here's to You, Bro.
